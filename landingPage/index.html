Save and Organize Your ChatGPT Conversations

ChatClip is a browser extension that lets you clip, save, and organize your ChatGPT conversations with ease.

Add to Chrome

Key Features

Discover the features that make ChatClip the perfect tool for managing your ChatGPT conversations.

Clip Conversations

Easily clip and save important parts of your ChatGPT conversations with a single click.

Organize with Folders

Create folders and subfolders to categorize your clips and keep everything organized.

Powerful Search

Quickly find specific clips using our powerful search functionality.

Tagging System

Add tags to your clips for even more granular organization and search capabilities.

Cloud Sync

Your saved clips are sync across all chrome browsers.

AI Powered

Automatically extracts text from conversation screenshots, creates titles, and organizes content intelligently.

How to Use ChatClip

Get started with ChatClip in just a few simple steps.

1

Start a Conversation

Begin chatting with ChatGPT as you normally would.

2

Enter you gemini API key

ChatClip uses gemini to extract text from conversation screenshots.

3

Take Screenshot and Upload or Use our Manual notes writing

ChatClip will automatically extract text from the screenshot and create a note.

4

Access Your Clips

View and manage your saved clips from the extension popup.
